About Chopta Chandrashila

Chopta is a serene hamlet in Uttarakhand offering majestic views of the Himalayas. Trekking to Tungnath and Chandrashila provides panoramic views of Nanda Devi, Trishul, and Chaukhamba.

  • Visit the highest Shiva temple - Tungnath
  • Summit the Chandrashila peak for 360° views
  • Enjoy the tranquil meadows and dense forests

Itinerary

Meet your guide, check-in, and relax amidst beautiful pine trees.

Hike to the scenic lake, set up camp and enjoy bonfire by the water.

Trail through dense forest, spot Himalayan birds, reach your camp at Chopta.

Early morning push to reach the temple and summit. Unforgettable views await!

Breakfast and departure. Carry memories for a lifetime.
