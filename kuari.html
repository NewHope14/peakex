About the Trek

Chopta Chandrashila trek offers breathtaking views of Himalayan peaks like Nanda Devi, Trishul, and Chaukhamba. The trail passes through forests of oak, deodar, and rhododendron, culminating in a panoramic summit that stirs the soul. Tungnath, the highest Shiva temple, makes this trail not just scenic, but spiritual.

Itinerary Snapshot

  • Day 1: Arrive at Sari Village and overnight stay
  • Day 2: Trek to Deoria Tal with lakeside camping
  • Day 3: Hike through dense forest to reach Chopta
  • Day 4: Summit Tungnath and Chandrashila Peak
  • Day 5: Departure after breakfast
